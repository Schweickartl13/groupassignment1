Suggestions for Routers

When looking for the right router there are many to choose from, here are a few of my suggestions to help start your decision.

Low-Budget Router

NETGEAR N750 Dual Band Wi-Fi Gigabit Router

This router is good for large Wi-Fi ranges, so it works well for a network for medium to large homes. This router has a lot of features and only cost about $68 from Amazon.

Features:
> has speeds pf 300+450 - 750Mbps speeds
> supports 802.11 a/b/g/n
> two USB 2.0 ports
> Quality of Service support
> Guest Network, VPN, and WPA/WPA2
> IPv6 compatible

Mid-Range Router

NETGEAR AC1750 Smart WiFi Router Dual Core 800MHz Processor

This router is a good balance between price and performance. It is still good for budget while still having some advance features that you would not get from a lower budget router. This router typically costs about $129 from Amazon.

Features:
> has speeds of 450+1300 Mbps
> supports 802.11 A/C and 802.11 a/b/g/n
> has 1 USB 2.0 port and 1 USB 3.0 port
> advaned Quality of Service
> Parental controls

High Performance Routers

D-Link AC3200 Ultra Wi-Fi Router (DIR-890L/R)

If you are looking for a router fpr performance and do not care about the budget as much this router has many advanced features. It is good for gaming and video streaming. This router costs about $230 from Amazon.

Features:
> supports speeds from 600-1300MBps
> supports 4K and HD video and online gaming simultaneously
> 1 USB 2.0 port and 1 3.0 USB 3.0 port
> supports 802.11 A/C and 802.11 a/b/g/n
> advance AC Smartbeam for enhanced Wi-Fi speed
> Quality of Service optimization
> advanced security features such as WPA/WPA2 encryption
