Jason's Suggestions for Routers

When looking for the right router there are many to choose from, here are a few of my suggestions to help start your decision.

Low-Budget Router

NETGEAR N300 WiFi Router

The N300 is the best ecnonomical choice it gets the job done for gaming and all your other basic needs. This is the same router i use for the price it's great and comes with netgear genie. With netgear genie it simplifys the controls you have with the router so even people new to technology can handle it,you can also download a nice mobile app for handling your router while away or fixing your mothers router while your away.


Features:
  • Price: $39.99
  • Ports: Five Fast Ethernet ports
  • NETGEAR genie
  • IPv6 compatible

Mid-Range Router

NETGEAR - AC750 Dual-Band Wi-Fi Router

The AC750 is a good middle ground if you have a little extra money for the dual band capabilities. With the dual band you can seperate your network so geusts can have access to the slower band and save the 5g for personnal use.
Features:
  • Price: $73.99
  • AC750 WiFi 300+433 Mbps speeds
  • Dual band capable
  • External 5dBi antenna
  • Gigabit Ethernet for best wired performance

High Performance Routers

NETGEAR Nighthawk X4S Smart WiFi Gaming Router

If money isn't really a issue than this router is a good choice, it is one of the more exspensive routers but also one of the best. It comes with a dual band 2.4GHz and 5GHz so you can section off the more demanding things with more bandwidth. It also includes 5 ethernet ports and two USB 3.0 ports
Features:
  • Price: $249.99
  • Ports: Five Gigabit Ethernet ports and Two USB 3.0 ports
  • Beamforming+
  • Next generation Wave2 WiFi
  • NETGEAR genie remote access
  • Speeds up to 2.53Gbps
  • Quad-Stream technology to stream to multiple devices simultaneously
